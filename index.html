About me

Builder. PM. Human.

I am a Senior Product Manager with 10+ years building products at scale — from payment rails processing crores of rupees daily, to consumer apps used by lakhs of users, to things I built at 2am just because an idea would not leave me alone.

I hold a CS degree from IIT Bombay and an MBA from IIM Ahmedabad, and I write real code in Python, Django, and React. I use AI tooling hands-on every day — not as a novelty, but as a genuine way to build and think faster.

I am equally comfortable at zero to one — finding the right problem and establishing early traction — and at one to hundred, where discipline, data, and execution determine whether a product wins.
